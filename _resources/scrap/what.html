By CONNECTING, MOBILIZING and INFLUENCING, the global rangelands program strengthens ILC members’ activities in-country and across its continental platforms.

The key focus areas help ILC members develop and strengthen partnerships with a common vision of tenure security for rangeland users; identify, develop and/or scale up innovative solutions to tenure challenges, assisting governments and other actors in implementing enabling policy and legislation; and through sharing experiences and lessons learned, infuence these actors in land tenure development and implementation.

Read Our Strategy

Connecting

ILC connects members with each other and with change-makers beyond the Coalition

Influencing

ILC influences key decision-makers, including governments, their partners and corporate actors

Mobilising

ILC mobilises members by reinforcing and equipping their efforts with the necessary knowledge, capacity, and opportunities

The Rangelands Initiative Global also supports a number of ongoing cross-cutting activities led by one or other of the global partners, and which involve ILC members and partners from the regions. These include:

Call for an International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists

Supporting the presentation of papers on rangelands at the annual WB Land Poverty Conference

Development of a webpage on RANGELANDS for the Land Portal Foundation

Development of a global statement on the status of land and resource tenure in rangelands

Contributing to a gap analysis of data and information on rangelands

Contributing to LANDMARK through populating the platform with information on community lands in rangelands
