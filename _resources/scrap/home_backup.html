International Land Coalition Rangelands Initiative

The ILC Rangelands Initiative is a global programme working to make rangelands more secure for local rangelands users. It contributes to the ILCâ€™s commitment-based initiatives diverse tenure systems and locally-managed ecosystems.

The ILC Rangelands Initiative works through three key mechanisms to reach its goal of more secure rangelands:

Connecting

developing and strengthening partnerships with a common vision of tenure security for local rangelands users

Influencing

sharing experiences and lessons learned with and between different actors in order to influence the development and implementation of supporting policy and legislation

Mobilizing

identifying, developing and supporting innovation solutions and their scaling-up in order to implement enabling policy and legislation

The ILC Rangelands Initiative supports the call for an International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists
